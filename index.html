B.Ed. First Semester Initiative

Campus Cleanliness Drive by B.Ed. First Semester Students: A Step Towards a Cleaner Institute

As part of our commitment to Swachh Bharat Abhiyan and community responsibility, the B.Ed. First Semester students of the Government Institute of Advanced Study in Education organized and actively participated in a cleanliness drive within the campus premises. This activity aimed to promote hygiene, environmental awareness, and a sense of ownership among future educators. The students cleaned classrooms, corridors, staff rooms, and surrounding areas of the institute. The initiative not only improved the campus environment but also instilled the values of civic sense, teamwork, and responsibility in teacher trainees. Such practical efforts are essential in shaping socially responsible educators who lead by example.
